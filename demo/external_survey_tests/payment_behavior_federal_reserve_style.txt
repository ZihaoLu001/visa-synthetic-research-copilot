Scenario: Payment behavior and mobile-payment trust interview guide.

1. Which payment method do you use most often for in-person purchases? Options: cash; debit card; credit card; mobile wallet; other
2. How often would you use a mobile wallet for everyday payments if this card made setup automatic?
3. Would fraud alerts and purchase protection increase your trust in this card?
4. Which payment pain point matters most to you? Options: fees; security or fraud; budgeting control; merchant acceptance; speed; privacy
5. What concern would stop you from using an automatic cheapest-payment recommendation? Options: privacy; wrong recommendation; loss of control; no clear savings; none
6. How likely would you be to use this card for online shopping?
7. What annual fee in CHF would you accept for payment optimization and protection features?
8. What would make this feel safer or more useful?
