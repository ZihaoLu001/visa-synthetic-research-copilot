Scenario: Payment proposition pricing and message test.

1. How likely would you be to adopt a payment assistant that helps you choose the most suitable payment method at checkout?
2. What annual fee in CHF would feel acceptable for this benefit bundle?
3. Which message is most convincing? Options: fee clarity; customer control; purchase protection; simpler checkout; fraud alerts
4. Which part of your life would this proposition fit best? Options: daily shopping; online shopping; travel payments; family budget; business expenses; none
5. What is the main barrier that would stop you from using this proposition?
6. Would making the service free for the first year change your likelihood to adopt it?
7. Which benefit would you remove first if the product needed to be simpler? Options: fee clarity; customer control; purchase protection; mobile-wallet support; fraud alerts
8. What should Visa or the issuing bank validate with real customers next?
